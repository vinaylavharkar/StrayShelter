WE ARE THEIR VOICES

The StrayShelter is the Stray animal adoption and welfare system. We offer services like adopting a stray animal, posting about stray animals for adoption, Registering as a veteirnary doctor to provide free service to injured and sick animals, seeking help from a registered veterinary doctor to treat the stray animals. We have created this Stray animals adoption and care system as part of our diploma course final Year Project. Our aim is to take forward this system and implement it to it's best.

StrayShelter: This project is an intiative to create public awareness for caring and adoption of stray animals. Developed for fulfillment of Capstone Project.
